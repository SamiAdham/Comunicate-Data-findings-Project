Indroduction¶

Bay Wheels is a regional public bicycle sharing system in the San Francisco Bay Area, California operated by Motivate in a partnership with the Metropolitan Transportation Commission.It was established as Bay Area Bike Share in August 2013. As of January 2018, the Bay Wheels system had over 2,600 bicycles in 262 stations across San Francisco, East Bay and San Jose.On June 28, 2017, the system officially re-launched as Ford GoBike in a partnership with Ford Motor Company.After Motivate's acquisition by Lyft, the system was subsequently renamed to Bay Wheels in June 2019.The system is expected to expand to 7,000 bicycles around 540 stations in San Francisco, Oakland, Berkeley, Emeryville, and San Jose.(Wikipedia)

Overall bike Rider usage¶

According to dataset we find biker rider usually rent the bike to go work the what below visulization shows.

1. Months: The demand will go to the peak in summer time from May-Oct 2018 becouse the winter is not a good time to rider a bike.
2. Weekdays: The demand will go to the peak in weekdays from Monday-Friday and the drop significantly in weekend.
3. Hours through the day: We have two waves of high demand first one in the early morning from 8-9 am the the second one from 17-18 pm.
4. Duration: Most of bike user rent bike between 8-10 min
All these parameters shows the worker effect the demand of ForBike System.
